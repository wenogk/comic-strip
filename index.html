Period.
The purpose of this comic is to normalize the idea of menstruation to kids. Every girl begin menstruation at one point in her life and it is very important to empower young girls by giving them the right resources. Menstruation sound scary and hence it has been always looked at as a taboo in different societies. By creating this project, we are hoping to normalize this topic in our cultures along the lines of introducing the idea for little kids and allowing young girls to express themselves freely.
